Holy Trinity AI Club

Welcome to the Holy Trinity AI Club! Our club is dedicated to learning more about the inner workings of AI, understanding how to utilize it, and demonstrating how it can be a disruptive technology across multiple fields.

We are also proud to be part of SAILea (Scholastic Artificial Intelligence League), a network of AI clubs across the country working towards a common goal: raising education about AI.

In our meetings, we'll explore the fundamentals of AI, including topics like an introduction to AI, neural networks, machine learning, computer vision, and much more. We'll also discuss how AI is revolutionizing various industries such as medicine, entertainment, finance, STEM, and beyond.

Join us in Mr. Bonanza's room (410) every other week after school from 3:00 to 4:00 PM to dive deep into the world of AI!

Sponsored by: Mr. Bonanza

Introduction to AI

An introduction to Artificial Intelligence where we discuss the basics, the history, and the current state of AI. This is a great starting point for anyone new to the subject.

Neural Networks

Learn how neural networks, a subset of machine learning, mimic the human brain's neurons to recognize patterns and solve complex problems.

Machine Learning

Explore how machines learn from data to improve their performance over time, a key component of modern AI applications.

Computer Vision

Delve into the field of computer vision, where AI is taught to interpret and make decisions based on visual data.

AI in Medicine

Discuss how AI is transforming the healthcare industry through applications like diagnostic tools, personalized treatment plans, and more.
